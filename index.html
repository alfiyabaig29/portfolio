📄 Research Paper · Seshadripuram College
Deepfake Technology: Threats, Detection, and Prevention
Alfiya Baig · Seshadripuram College, Bengaluru
Abstract

A comprehensive review of deepfake technology — AI-generated synthetic media using GANs and autoencoders — examining how these techniques have evolved, why detection is increasingly difficult, and what can be done to counter misuse. Evaluates current deep-learning detection methods and emerging multi-modal verification approaches, while addressing the ethical, legal, and societal dimensions of the problem.
